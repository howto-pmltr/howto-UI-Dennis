HowTo

minimal. informative

By alleviating the pain of lacking knowledge, we will give every individual on the planet the assets to learn how to do anything

Concern of lack of skill and knowledge in a certain task?

HowTo app provides variety of information in various categories and skills!

Trying to solve a problem but need a proper guidance or steps?

HowTo app provides a resource for users to find, share, and create tutorials and “how-to”s on various tasks and problems.

Features

  • Welcoming Interface for new users and registered users
  • Easy sign-up
  • Easily add a how-to guide
  • Write a guide and choose when guide is published, otherwise it stays in draft.
  • Tag guides and filter through guides with keywords
  • Edit and delete post

Technologies

React, redux, logger, thunk, and material-ui

Express, knex, pg, jsonwebtoken, bcryptjs, jest, supertest
